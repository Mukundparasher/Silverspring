Silver Spring School

(Mango,Jamshedpur,Jharkhand)

Mission

As an institute, Silver Spring is committed to provide a stable, caring and supportive family-like environment, where students can receive mature guidance through daily interaction with able faculty and become accountable, productive, and ethical citizens with the skills to think innovatively, reason critically, communicate effectively, and respect people of other cultures.

Vision

To become a premiere school of education recognized for its diversity, and known for excellence in teaching, learning and research.
