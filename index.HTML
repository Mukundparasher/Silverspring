Our Vision.

Mission

As an Play school , Silver Spring is committed to provide a stable, caring and supportive family-like environment , where students can receive mature guidance through daily interaction with able faculty and become accountable , productive , and ethical citizens with the skills to think innovatively, reason critically , communicate effectively , and respect peoples of other cultures. . The school admits boys and girls from the age group of 3 and 1/2 years and above. It has modern Kindergarten equipment. The teachers are highly qualified and trained.
